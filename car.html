Nissan Tidda 2014

The Nissan Tiida (Japanese: 日産・ティーダ, Hepburn: Nissan Tīda) is a compact car produced by the Japanese manufacturer Nissan since 2004 through three generations.
Depending on the market, the Tiida was also marketed as a subcompact car, particularly in the Americas for the first-generation model as the Nissan Versa.
